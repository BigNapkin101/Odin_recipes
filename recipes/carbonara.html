Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Description!

Pasta carbonara is an incredibly simple yet delicious dish consisting of ingredients we all love. for everything from leftover lunch to a weekend dinner. Carbonara is a pasta dish with a creamy consistency. This sauce is made with bacon, garlic, pasta water, cheese and eggs(optional). You can always use vegan replacement options for this dish, don't be afraid to switch it up a bit! This is an easy recipe anyone can make with a little effort.

Ingredients

  • Bacon (pancetta if you're able to find)
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Spaghetti (Or your favorite!)
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Add some bacon and water to a skillet and let it simmer

  2. Continue to simmer until the water has evaporated

  3. Keep cooking the bacon until crispy

  4. Remove the bacon from the pan and save the bacon fat

  5. Saute the garlic in the same skillet until golden

  6. Add the garlic to a bowl with 1 tablespoon of bacon fat, eggs, egg yolk, Parmesan and pepper

  7. Mix the ingredients together

  8. Cook your pasta of choice until al dente

  9. Drain your pasta and save 1 cup of the water

  10. Slowly, pour the hot cooking water to the egg mixture

  11. Pour the mixture over your pasta and give it a toss to coat

  12. Add some of that bacon, crumbled or however you'd like

  13. Let the pasta rest for a bit and keep tossing to coat

  14. When the sauce has thickened, serve with some parsley

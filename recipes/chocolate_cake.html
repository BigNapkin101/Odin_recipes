Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a staple in all households. Whatever someone might not like, I guarantee they like chocolate cake. Here we have a fantastic recipe that is ensured to keep you wanting more. This relatively simple recipe should help you get started with a delicious dessert as a finish for those other recipes you must've tried earlier.

Ingredients!

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Espresso powder
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Boiling water

Steps!

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit

  2. Prep two 9-inch cake pans or similar

  3. Butter the inside or spray it and lightly flour it

  4. To a bowl, add; flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder.

  5. Whisk through or combine well until fully mixed.

  6. Add the wet ingredients to the dry mix; milk, vegetable oil, eggs and vanilla

  7. Keep combining while carefully adding boiling water to the batter until well combined

  8. Distribute the cake batter evenly between the two pans

  9. Bake for about 30-35 minutes

  10. Keep checking readiness with a toothpick (or something similar) until it comes out clean

  11. Remove from the oven and make sure to let it cool for about 10 minutes

  12. Frost with whatever you'd like!

  13. Serve! Enjoy!
